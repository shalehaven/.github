# Shalehaven Partners Energy & Asset Management

**Shalehaven Partners** is a private energy investment and asset management firm specializing in tax-advantaged, non-operated oil & gas projects in proven U.S. basins. We partner with best-in-class operators — including Hunt Oil, Admiral Permian, Aethon Energy, Devon Energy, COG Operating, and Spur Energy Partners — to deliver disciplined, data-driven returns to our investors.

---

## 🛢️ What We Do

- **Non-Operated Oil & Gas Investments** — We acquire working interests in high-quality shale and conventional plays, leveraging operator expertise while managing capital efficiently.
- **Production Analytics** — We track and forecast production across our portfolio using type curves, decline curve analysis, and real-time data integration.
- **Financial Operations** — We manage AFE (Authorization for Expenditure), JIB (Joint Interest Billing), and revenue accounting to maintain full transparency with investors.
- **Geospatial & Infrastructure Analysis** — We perform line-of-sight and pad/pipeline screening to support drilling decisions and capital planning.
